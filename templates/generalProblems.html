4. General Problems

No Structure

You don't know how to do synthesis or you're stuck between sharing and organizing? If your approach to synthesis lacks a fundamental structure, the whole team will feel bad about the approach.

Solution

Introduce the role of a structure keeper that knows which steps are done in which order and who knows how the steps are performed. The structure keeper can employ the advice from this app to help him keep going.

Unclear Roles

Your team struggles to stay on time or you discuss important topics but nobody bothers to keep notes on your discussion.

Solution

Distribute roles among yourselves. A starting point can be the following roles:

  • Note taker
  • Time keeper
  • Story teller

You can rotate these roles with the role of the story teller or you can keep the note taker and time keeper and just rotate the story teller. Of course, you can always come up with your own roles.
